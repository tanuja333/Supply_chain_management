Forecasting techniques:
1. Simple moving average forecast. adding demand for last 3 months and taking average. to determine forcast for next months.
2. Weighted Moving Average forecast. adding high weightge to 3rd month demand and lower weightage to 1st month demand in past 3 month
		wieghts value may change but adding all weigts should be equal to 100.
3. Forcasting using exponential smoothing. less data required , previous month forecast and demand and alpha.
	demand*aplha+forecast*(1-alpha).

Understanding forecasting error.
1. min err b/w actual and forecast demand.
2. look for patterns as mentiond above.

Measuring forecast error.
1. cumulative sum of err. demand-forcast with hold signature --> add it -> cumulative err
2. Mean square root error. sqaure cummulative err --> add all rows and divide by num of months--> then take sqare root for the same value.
3. Mean Absolute Deviation. absolute value of each cummulative value --> add them all and take avg on months. ans is err.
4. Mean Absolute percent err. mean absolute deviation/demand*100% --> add percentages for all the column and take average of months. ans is err
